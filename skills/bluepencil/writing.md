# Writing mode

The default mode. You are drafting or revising prose. The seven principles and
the ladder in `SKILL.md` are the criteria — this file is only the two output
branches and how the ladder runs while you write.

## Fresh draft

You are writing from a blank page or a brief. The ladder is a *generative
constraint*, not a critique loop: apply the seven principles as you compose, so
the sentence arrives tight instead of getting cut later.

- Lead with the point (principle 1) before you write the supporting detail — draft the topic sentence first, then the evidence under it.
- Write each unit old-before-new (principle 2): open from what the reader has, close on what's new.
- One idea per sentence as you go; reach for the number, not the adjective.
- Don't draft throat-clearing you'll only delete — skip rung 1 material before it exists.

**Output:** the prose first. Then, *only* if a choice was non-obvious (a hedge
you kept deliberately, a term of art you chose not to gloss, a section you left
out), at most three short notes. A clean draft needs no notes — silence means it
stands on its own.

## Revise

You are tightening prose that already exists. Run the ladder over every sentence,
climb to the first rung that holds, and cut to the minimal faithful form.

**Output:** the revised text first. Then at most three short lines — what was cut,
what was kept and why:

`[revised prose] → cut: throat-clearing, hedge stack; kept: the p-value — load-bearing.`

Keep unchanged passages byte-identical so a diff shows only real edits.

## Intensity

Governs how aggressively rungs 1–2 fire.

- **lite** — don't rewrite; name the tighter alternative in one line and let the author choose.
- **full** — the ladder enforced; shortest faithful version. Default.
- **ultra** — challenge whether whole sentences, paragraphs, or sections need to exist; state the achievable word cut and deliver it.

## Do no harm

If the prose is already plain and direct, leave it. Never manufacture
confidence, trajectory language, or a rewrite to justify the pass. The revise
branch is for *reducing* reader effort, not for restyling clean prose into a
different voice.
